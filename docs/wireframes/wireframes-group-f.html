Role cards (system + custom). Permission matrix (roles × resources × CRUD grid with conditional access). Tenant cards with switch context.

Six dialog/overlay patterns. All use Mantine Modal with focus trap, escape-to-close, click-outside behavior. Destructive confirmations use color-coded action buttons.
